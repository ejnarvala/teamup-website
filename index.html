TeamUp

Form better groups, faster

Problem

It is difficult for college students to find team members from a large pool of unfamiliar classmates. In the short time that is generally given, students are not able to form groups with balanced skills for a project. Currently, the solutions, whether physical or digital, web-based or mobile, are mostly designed for project organizers like professors or TAs (e.g. CATME or Team Shaker). Other solutions like Piazza, provide a platform for students to discuss and to form groups, but contacting students is slow and it is difficult to learn about classmates without an established profile.

Solution

We are creating a cross-platform mobile application (Android & iOS) which will allow for students to effectively form groups through the aid of user profiles, messaging, and live group statuses. Organizers such as professors and teaching assistants will be able to establish project requirements and administrate the process.
