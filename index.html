TeamUp

Find your best teammates

Problem Description

It is uneasy for college students to find the appropriate team members from a pool of unfamiliar classmates. In the short time, students are not able to form groups with balanced skills to cover all required skills for a project. Currently, the solutions, whether physical or digital, web-based or mobile, are mostly designed for project organizers like professors or TAs like CATME or Team Shaker. Other solutions like Piazza, provide a platform for students to discuss group formation but the group status can not be maintained well.

Our solution

We plan to develop a web-based and a Android application for organizers like professors and TAs to handle project and students to handle group forming in a project. Organizers can release new projects and set the maximum and minimum size of groups. Students can check other studentsâ€™ profile and see whether there are good teammates or any team with absence.
